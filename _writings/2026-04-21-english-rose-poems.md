## **Who**

There is a woman  
I have never met  
who lives in me.

I have loved her  
in a hundred different bodies.

I learned to want her  
before I knew  
what wanting was.

If I ever loved her  
or if  
I only wanted  
to dissolve  
into something  
that did not carry  
my weight



## **Many Faces**

I carried her  
for years  
without naming her

in the women I noticed,  
in the stories I remembered.

I have seen her  
many times  
in different eyes,  
in different lives.

Why I follow,  
I do not know.



## **Root**

Somewhere early  
something stayed:

a softness  
I have never escaped.

Not because I must.  
Not because I chose.

Somewhere  
beneath the rose.



## **Ruin**

I would give you my soul,  
but it’s not whole.

And you  
too warm  
for something cold.

Love you in ways  
that ruins rest.

I’d give you me,  
but “me” is war.

You want a home.  
I am nowhere to stay.
