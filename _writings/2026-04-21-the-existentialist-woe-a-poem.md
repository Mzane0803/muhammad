Rarely considered in youth’s carefree stride,  
Yet into its depths, my gaze began to slide.  
In the void’s embrace, where silence reigns supreme,  
Its gaze returned, unraveling reality’s seam.  
It speaks of the emptiness in our earthly dance,  
In love’s brief spark, in hate’s fiery glance,  
In tears of mourning, in fleeting joys, we clasp,  
In the sting of agony, in euphoria’s gasp.

This is my existential trial, in shadows cast,  
Seeking meaning in a universe so vast.  
Through existential gloom, this journey I roam,  
In pursuit of life’s essence, this truth I own.
