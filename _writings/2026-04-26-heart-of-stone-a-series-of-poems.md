*The Execution of Lady Jane Grey*



&nbsp;

&nbsp;

O heart of stone,  
What hast thou won?

No wound may enter thee

But neither  
the dawn.

---

A hardened heart  
does not break

It simply ceaseth  
to open.

Calls this strength,  
and survives itself.

---

Some hearts grow stone

Too many winters.  
Too many hands.

When morning comes,                                                                                                                                                     they turn away

There are gardens                                                                                                                                                             the dawn shall never bless.
