King Henry V and his weary soldiers

What does a man have?

If he has no pride,

What does a man have?

If he has no courage,

What does a man have?

If he trembles before fear,

What does a man have?

If he has never loved,

What does a man have?

If he has never been wounded,

What does a man have?

If his life is empty

what does a man have

but a life full of sorrow?

a man has nothing

but the quiet misery

of an unlived life.
